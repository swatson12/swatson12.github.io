3D Model

Otago Polytechnic Bachelor of IT - Multimedia Development - Semester 2 2017

When I was trying to think of an idea for this project, I couldn't decide how or what classroom to "redesign". Adon has spent the last couple of weeks scratching his head, wondering how he's going to fit all the new Project Ones into the Project Room next semester. So I decided to try to redesign the Project Room.

The planning for this was definitely non-existent. When I was making the model, I worked on it periodically, adding to it whenever I felt like it needed improvement. My only plan was to redesign the Project Room.

I used a couple of assets that people had made and uploaded into the 3D Warehouse integrated into SketchUp.

  • The computers
  • The TVs

The rest of the model I did myself with the tools in SketchUp. Once I got the hang of the way SketchUp works and all the shortcuts that it has, I was away laughing, making a model that is a lot better than I expected it to be.
