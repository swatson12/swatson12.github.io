About the Portfolio

This portfolio came to be because I had to make one for Software Engineering in the first semester of 2017. I spent a night making the inital design with the home page and how all the pages are laid out. Ever since I made it, I've just been adding to it and refining it to make it as good as possible. I have also made it responsive, so I can view it on my iPhone with no issues.

I designed the website to have borders that don't connect because I had made a decision that I didn't like the way it looked when they were connected, it made it feel too boxed in and rigid. The disconnected lines makes it feel less constrained to me.
